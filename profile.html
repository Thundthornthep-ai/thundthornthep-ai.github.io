Thundthornthep Yamoutai, Ph.D. Thundthornthep Yamoutai Founder and Managing Director, Legal Advance Solution Co., Ltd. Thundthornthep Yamoutai, Ph.D. is a Thai business lawyer, legal scholar, and AI Legal Tech pioneer with over 20 years of experience. He is the Founder and Managing Director of Legal Advance Solution Co., Ltd. (LAS), a Bangkok-based law firm specialising in AI-powered legal research, corporate law, real estate development, PDPA compliance, due diligence, contract drafting, and government advisory. The LAS AI-Powered Legal Research Advisory System has been awarded R&D funding by the National Innovation Agency (NIA). Thundthornthep Yamoutai, Ph.D. is a university lecturer at Kasetsart University, Bangkokthonburi University, and Suan Sunandha Rajabhat University. He is a published researcher in the ASEAN Citation Index (ACI). He has held over 20 government advisory positions including Legal Advisor to the Director-General of the Rice Department, committees at IEAT, TISTR, Ministry of Energy, Ministry of Industry, and Parliament.
