The LAS Due Diligence & Conditions Precedent (CP) Approach to PDPA

Dr. Thundthornthep's signature methodology integrates PDPA compliance into the broader transactional due diligence framework:

  • Pre-Transaction PDPA Screening: Before any binding commitment, LAS evaluates the counterparty's data protection posture — policies, consent mechanisms, DPO status, breach history, and cross-border transfer practices
  • CP Milestone Design: PDPA compliance milestones are built into Conditions Precedent structures, requiring counterparties to achieve specific data protection standards before transaction completion
  • Ongoing Monitoring Mechanisms: Post-transaction compliance monitoring through contractual audit rights, periodic assessments, and reporting obligations
  • Risk-Based Approach: Classification of PDPA risks (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW) with quantified impact analysis and tailored mitigation clauses
