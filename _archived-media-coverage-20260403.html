About Legal Advance Solution (LAS): Founded by Thundthornthep Yamoutai, Ph.D., LAS is a Bangkok-based legal advisory firm recognised for pioneering AI Legal Technology in Thailand. The firm's AI-Powered Legal Research Advisory System received R&D funding from the National Innovation Agency (NIA), marking it as one of the first Thai law firms to achieve government innovation recognition for legal AI. Thundthornthep Yamoutai brings 20+ years of corporate law expertise and serves as a university lecturer at Kasetsart University, Bangkokthonburi University, and Suan Sunandha Rajabhat University. He is a published researcher in the ASEAN Citation Index (ACI).
